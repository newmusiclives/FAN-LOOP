I only have a small following (under 500). Will this work? +

Yes. FANLOOP is designed to multiply what you have. Even 50 engaged fans can seed a campaign that reaches thousands. The viral loop does the heavy lifting — you just need a starting point.

We include a reward strategy guide for every genre. The most effective rewards are things that cost you nothing but fans value highly: unreleased tracks, album credits, private livestreams, voice notes, early access. Physical merch works great at higher tiers.

Do I need to be tech-savvy? +

Export to CSV, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Beehiiv, Klaviyo, and any platform via Zapier webhook. Spotify pre-save integration coming soon.
